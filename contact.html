What types of projects do you work on?

I build AI-based cybersecurity systems and full-stack web applications focused on real-time threat detection, security automation, and intelligent data analysis.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. Simple websites typically take 2-3 weeks, while complex web applications can take 6-12 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

I primarily work remotely and have experience collaborating with teams across different time zones. I'm also available for on-site work or hybrid arrangements depending on project needs and location.
