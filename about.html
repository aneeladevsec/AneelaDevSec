Who I Am

I’m Aneela Ameen, a Cybersecurity & AI enthusiast with a strong academic and professional background. I graduated in 2024 from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur (Bahawalnagar Campus) with a CGPA of 3.94/4.0, securing a Gold Medal for top performance in Cyber Security.

My journey started with a passion for technology and evolved into working across multiple domains including Full Stack Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity. I began my professional career at Lumintro as a Full Stack Developer, where I worked on real-world applications and intelligent systems.

Alongside my job, I have been actively freelancing since 2019, working with both direct and international clients, delivering secure, scalable, and AI-powered solutions. I have also collaborated with different companies on project-based roles, gaining hands-on experience in solving real industry problems.

In January 2026, I joined Tech Trego (USA) as an AI/ML Course Instructor, where I teach and mentor students in modern AI technologies. I also have professional experience in cybersecurity domains including penetration testing, OSINT, and security analysis.

I continuously invest in learning and growth. I have completed hands-on training in AI, Cybersecurity, and SOC operations from Infosec Institute, along with certifications and learning from platforms like Google, Cisco, Udemy, and Red Team programs.

My goal is to build intelligent, AI-powered cybersecurity systems that can detect, prevent, and respond to modern digital threats — shaping a safer and smarter digital future.
